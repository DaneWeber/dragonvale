# The Mask of Verdigriss

This item can cloak the unseen in a dancing violet corona. It can sense magic, and is offended by the aetheric reek of undeath. It can grant its wearer the strength to carry on after taking heavy wounds.

(Int 10, Wis 18, Cha 18, Ego 17) 120 foot darkvision, blindsense, and hearing
