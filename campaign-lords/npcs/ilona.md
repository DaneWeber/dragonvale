# Ilona

## Description

Ilona is a star, a famous dancer and acrobat, renowned for her beauty as well as her skill. Few of her fans, however, know that she is also a master thief, a notorious phantom responsible for a series of thefts which have baffled investigators and panicked wealthy collectors.

Ilona is the daughter of a wealthy merchant. She was raised with the best of everything, with a fine education and finer clothes, but her rebellious nature brought her into conflict with her mother's plans. Though she was expected to follow her mother into trade, Ilona dreamed of a more glamorous life. Turning her back on her family and peers, she left home as a youth to see the world and find the adventure she craved. The debutant soon put her upper-class training to a new use, earning a living as a dancer. Winning acclaim for her skill, she soon fell in with a troupe of entertainers. She spent the next few years as a part of the roving show, honing her skills and perfecting her art It was then that Ilona began to learn how travailing mountebanks supplement their earning; with confidence games and theft.

Relishing the challenge and excitement, as well as the treasure, the young woman employed her enormous talent in crime. Learning skills from veteran acrobats and cat-burglars, Ilona became an accomplished thief. As the dancer's fame grew, she performed at ever greater venues, and was invited to private audiences with the nobility. All the while, the celebrated beauty continued to practice her clandestine hobby. Leaping over rooftops and slithering through high windows, no strong-room was safe.

Ilona is a small woman, with blue-black hair and jade-green eyes which twinkle with mischief.. At 22, she is already a veteran acrobat and a master thief, but not a warrior. If discovered somewhere she does not belong, she usually tries to avoid a fight, dodging attacks and running as soon as possible, trusting in the masks she always wears to keep her identity safe. However, her blinding reflexes and liquid movements make her a formidable opponent when cornered.
