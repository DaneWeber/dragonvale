# Welcome to the Dragonvale

This wiki is for content and information regarding the Dragonvale D&D campaign. Players and the DM can feel free to add or edit any content as they see fit. For our purpose, anything added to this wiki is considered in-game public knowledge unless specified otherwise. You can take advantage of this wiki to add background information on your character or unique items, details on your canton, or stuff about old quests.

Bad in-character poetry will not be tolerated and will be removed.
