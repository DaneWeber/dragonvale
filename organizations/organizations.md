# Dragonvale Organizations

## Adventurer's Guild

The Adventurer's Guild is the only official guild within Hallstadt. All orphanages in the Dragonvale are operated by the guild. The guild also operates guild halls in most of the cities of Hallstadt. These halls operate as hiring agencies for mercenary work, training centers for martial arts, and storage rooms for rent. The halls often provide a sort of museum section as well.

## Glorious and Humble Ancient Order of Dreadnought Warriors of Righteous Light

(Order of Light)

This is the order of paladins. Every paladin must submit to the authority of two heads: the temporal ruler and the spiritual ruler. As such, in order to rightly act as a paladin, each member must have fealty to a temporal lord who grants him temporal authority, and each must submit to a clerical confessor who supervises spiritual acts. Paladins never seek lordship or ultimate rule. Permitting such an eventuality allows for wicked ambition.

The Heironean Code consists of three sets of duties:

- **Duty to the People.** This duty stresses courage, justice, mercy, valor, protection of the weak, and faithfulness to church superiors or officers of righteous law.

- **Duty to the Deities.** This duty stresses obedience to Heironeous himself, devotion to the church, proper worship, generosity, championing good against evil, and putting the needs of the church and the faith above those of mortals.

- **Duty to a Lady.** This duty pertains to the concept of courtly love, devotion to one's beloved, and respect toward all women in general. Female paladins suffer by this duty, as they may only be rightly courted by a man of greater martial prowess.

Paladins have a tripartite obedience: obedience to a spiritual director in matters of conscience and the spirit; obedience to a ruler in matters of war and temporal authority; and obedience to superiors within the Order in matters of conduct, training, and the operations of the order.

## Orichalcum

A vast and powerful secret society of unknown organization and intentions. Verdigriss, Lord Jura and Master of the Zircon, is rumored to be a member.
