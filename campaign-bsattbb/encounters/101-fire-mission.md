### The Prisoners

**From the Village of Frostholm:**

1. **Grandmother Astrid Kolbrun** (68) - Village elder and lorekeeper. Speaks Giant fluently - she was captured by frost giants as a young woman decades ago and lived among them for three years before escaping. Wears a tattered wool shawl.

2. **Bjorn Ironfoot** (45) - Blacksmith, Astrid's son. Strong but badly beaten during capture - has a broken arm crudely splinted.

3. **Liv Ironfoot** (42) - Bjorn's wife.

4. **Erik Ironfoot** (12) - Bjorn and Liv's eldest son. Trying to be brave but terrified. Has a wooden carving of a bear his father made him hidden in his pocket.

5. **Little Sigrid Ironfoot** (7) - Their daughter. Clutches a torn rag doll.

6. **Katrin the Weaver** (34) - Widow.

7. **Harald Longstride** (52) - Hunter and tracker. Injured leg.

8. **Young Olaf** (15) - Harald's nephew, apprentice hunter.

9. **Father Mikkel** (61) - Village priest of Yondolla. Wears a small wooden holy symbol under his shirt.

10. **Runa Frostwhisper** (29) - Ranger who was travelling near the village. Drugged and bound more securely than the others.

11. **Torsten the Silent** (40) - Simple-minded but gentle woodcutter. Very strong. Doesn't fully understand what's happening but protective of the children.
