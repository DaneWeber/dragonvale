# Businesses

**Bristly Bugbear** (King's Keep) - Friendly tavernkeeper has provided meals to the new Lord Protector and his companions.

**Barret Poyn's Carpentry** (King's Keep) - Carpenter plying his trade in a former coffin-making shop near the Ironbar Cemetery.

**King's House** (outside King's Keep) - Large manor and estate that has turned into an inn, dock, and trading post.
