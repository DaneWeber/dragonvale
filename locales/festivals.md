# Festivals

## Night of Torches

On the night of the new moon, creatures of shadow and darkness are liable to prowl. Throughout the globe, people have different ways to warding away the darkness. A clear, cloudless sky keeps away most of the shadow creatures, but if clouds, fog, or storms darken the sky, untold terrors may roam. In the Dragonvale, everyone keeps candles and torches burning throughout the night in their homes. The cities already keep street-lamps lit, but on the Night of Torches they set out an over-abundance of lamps and torches, especially along the city walls. Because the light can make sleep difficult, many parties are held late into the night and dawn, against the recommendations of the Pelorites.

The next Night of Torches is 13 days after the affair with Barret Poyn's basement.

## Blasingdell Festival

The city of Blasingdell holds an annual competition among musicians. This competition has given rise to a week-long festival that draws visitors from outside the Dragonvale. This festival is held during the first week of Fairyfeet.

The festival begins 39 days after the affair with Barret Poyn's basement.
